Reimagined Machines CVPR 2024 · AI Art Gallery

AI-generated robots reprocessed with classical computer-vision algorithms into a striking pop-art aesthetic. By randomizing prompt features and tuning thresholding parameters, each machine takes on a distinct persona — exploring the interplay between established vision techniques, generative AI, and the evolving relationship between humans and machines.

Autoregressive Mosaics CVPR 2026 · AI Art Gallery

An attempt to force a text-only LLM to paint, one discrete pixel at a time. Given a blank grid and a prompt, the model must infer where to place each character and color using only its linguistic training — exposing how these models represent, and often fracture, everyday visual concepts and geometry.
